Help & Support Center

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I create a new trip?

After logging in, go to the "My Trips" section on the homepage and click the '+' button. A pop-up will appear where you can enter your destination and travel dates.

Can I edit a trip after creating it?

Yes! Simply click on any trip card in the "My Trips" section, and you will be taken to the trip details page where you can add places of interest and remarks for each day.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login page, click the "Forgotten your password?" link. You will be guided through the steps to reset it.
